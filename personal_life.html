Personal Life

Hockey

I've been playing hockey since I was 8 years old, and it's one of my favorite things to do. There's nothing like having the wind rush past you as you fly down the ice and shoot the puck into the net, and it's a feeling I can't replicate anywhere else. Below is a video of me scoring a goal in youth league when I was a kid!

Tennis

I love playing tennis in my free time! I played all through high school and being on the court with my friends and teammates are my fondest memories from that time. It's a beautiful game and I'm grateful that I've been able to play it at a high level.

Working Out

My go-to method of blowing off steam and relieving stress is lifting weights in the gym. I've been consistently working out for more than 2 years now, and it's amazing to see how I've gotten stronger and healthier, both physically and mentally, as I've stuck with it.

Piano

I enjoy playing piano in my free time. I love listening to music, and it's even better when I can listen to music that I'm playing myself! I took lessons throughout my childhood and while I haven't mastered it, I know enough where I can pick up any song if I practice. My favorite piece to play is "Nuvole Bianche" by Ludovico Einaudi. I also enjoy playing Christmas music during the holidays.

TV and Video Games

I like to relax by watching TV and playing video games. My favorite shows to watch are sitcoms like The Big Bang Theory and Modern Family because they're easy to watch and relax to after a long day. I also like playing video games in my spare time. My favorites are sports games like NHL or MLB The Show and I also like to play Star Wars games.

Books

Books help me learn and also let my imagination run wild. One of my favorite things to do is read fiction, usually fantasy, as I wind down before I go to sleep at night. It gets me off my phone and allows me lose myself in a different world. My favorites are the Ranger's Apprentice and Brotherband series from John Flanagan and the Inheritance Cycle from Christopher Paolini. These books were my childhood and hold a special place in my heart, and I'll usually come back and reread them every few years because I love the journey so much.
